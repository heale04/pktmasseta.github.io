Saturday, Sep 05
12:30PM-2:30PM
Roof Deck BBQ
Here at PKT, we like to start things off right. Come enjoy burgers and cold drinks on our roof deck while overlooking the Boston skyline.
2:30PM-6:00PM
Smoothies, Wings, and Games
Who doesn't love fresh smoothies and delicious wings? Take a tour of our awesome house, meet our brothers, and enjoy our in-house entertainment!
6:30PM-8:30PM
Lobster and Clam Chowder
Our in-house chef serves up thick and creamy authentic Boston "chowda" and juicy lobsters cooked to perfection. Enjoy a classic dinner with Boston's finest seafood before the night gets crazy!
10:00PM-12:30AM
PKT Midnight Beach Party
You won't want to miss our kickoff party: sick beats, tropical drinks, and one of the hottest DJs in Boston! Stay late to chill with the bros, grab some food, and enjoy our afterparty.
Sunday, Sep 06
10:00AM-1:00PM
Yard Games on Kresge Oval
After a night of hard partying, spend your morning relaxing and playing games. Join us on Kresge for frisbee, football, and other yard games.
1:00PM-6:00PM
Paintball with PKT
Come join us for an all out war on the battlegrounds of PKT. Squad up with your fellow prefrosh and put all those countless hours on CoD to use!
4:30PM-6:00PM
Backyard Baseball
An old classic with a twist: see how far that fruit can fly! Join us behind our house to absolutely destroy all the fruit we have... with a baseball bat!
6:00PM-7:30PM
Steak Dinner
Steak. Dinner. Need I say more?
7:30PM-10:30PM
PKT Poker Tournament
Every year PKT has the hottest poker tournament, run by the founders of MIT's Poker Bots competition. Be one of the last men standing and take home an Apple Watch, Jambox, or a $50 Amazon gift card; knock out brothers along the way for hundreds in gift cards! How many bounties can you collect?
10:30PM-2:00AM
Party Hopping and Chinatown Run
Take the edge off after an intense night of poker. We'll hit the hottest parties and end the night with a trip to one of our favorite destinations for late-night food: Chinatown.
Monday, Sep 07
12:00PM-2:00PM
Fire and Ice
All you can eat stir fry? Hell yes. Join us for lunch at a house favorite.
2:30PM-4:30PM
Trampoline Dodgeball at SkyZone
We're taking a school yard classic to the next level. Dodge, duck, dip, dive, and dodge in a warehouse covered wall-to-wall with trampolines.
6:00PM-7:30PM
Epic Meal Time
We like to chill, we like to grill. After 72 hours of going hard at rush, take it easy on our roof deck and check out our amazing view of Boston.
8:00PM-11:00PM
Laser Tag and Frosties
Oh walk of shame, walk of game. What up! Head to the local arcade area and have fun shooting lasers at middle school kids. Then top off the evening with Wendy's classic Frosties.
Tuesday, Sep 08
6:00PM-7:30PM
Shish Kabobs, Butterbeers, and Game of Thrones
Enter a world of betrayal, mystery, and death with HBO's Game of Thrones. Sit back and enjoy the fantasy as you feast on your Westerosi Shish Kabobs and sip on non-alcoholic homemade butterbeers.
8:00PM-11:00PM
Miracle Berry and Froyo
You've never had a trip like this. We're going to turn your taste buds inside out as we make sour become sweet. Get ready to experience food like never before.
Wednesday, Sep 09
9:00PM-1:30AM
Bowling and IHOP
This is where the all-American sport of bowling meets the International House of Pancakes. Clearly the best combination of sport and food outside the competitive hotdog scene.
