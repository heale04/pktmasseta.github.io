  • About

    The Mass Eta chapter of Phi Kappa Theta is an MIT fraternity located in the beautiful Back Bay area of Boston. We are brotherhood of gentlemen, commited to living by the four ideals of our fraternity through the execution of our motto:
    "Give, Expecting Nothing Thereof"

  • Rush

    Every fall we invite MIT students to explore our fraternity through the Rush process. We hold a ton of great events during Rush week to give us a chance to meet new people and help them decide if they would like to be a part of our brotherhood.
